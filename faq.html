Troubleshooting

Problems sending or receiving messages

The most common reason why you can't send or receive i2Talk messages is a bad Internet connection. If you're sure your device is connected to the Internet, there are a few reasons why your i2Talk messages aren't going through:

  • Your phone needs to be restarted or turned off and on.
  • You haven't completed the initial verification process.
